Instructions

Follow the LPU-approved process to collect your data

We designed these steps based on LPU Touch and UMS so you can grab marks, credits, and semester details without confusion.

Step 1

LPU Touch – View your marks

  1. Open the LPU Touch app and sign in with your credentials.
  2. Tap on "View Marks" from the main dashboard.
  3. Select the semester you want to calculate TGPA for.
  4. Note down each subject’s credits, grade point, and total marks.

What you’ll see

  • Attendance marks
  • Continuous assessment
  • Mid-term score
  • End-term score
Step 2

UMS Portal – Subject credits

  1. Visit ums.lpu.in and log in.
  2. Find Important Links → LMS.
  3. Navigate to Program Schedules.
  4. Select your program & term to see subject credits.

Use this data to

  • Verify course titles
  • Confirm credit count
  • Check L-T-P structure
Step 3

Calculate TGPA

  1. Add each subject in the TGPA section.
  2. Enter credits and grade points (e.g., O=10, A+=9).
  3. Click Calculate TGPA.

Formula

(Σ Credits × Grade Point) ÷ Σ Credits

Step 4

Calculate CGPA

  1. Collect SGPA and credits for each semester.
  2. Add them in the CGPA section.
  3. Click Calculate CGPA for the final result.

Formula

(Σ Credits × SGPA) ÷ Σ Credits

Student Guide

Advanced tips to boost your grades

Go beyond the calculator with targeted strategies. Use these to plan assessments and communicate with your mentors.

Set a Target TGPA

Before mid-terms, estimate your required grade points. Use the TGPA calculator to test scenarios like 9.0 or 9.5 and set study milestones.

Track Credits Carefully

LPU electives and labs carry different credits. Verify every subject in UMS to avoid miscalculations.

Review Weightage

Continuous assessment can impact final grades heavily. Compare your CA score with attendance to spot quick improvements.

Plan for Scholarships

Many LPU scholarships rely on maintaining a minimum CGPA. Use the CGPA calculator to check future requirements.
